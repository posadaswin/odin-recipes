Description

Picture a symphony of flavors and textures, lovingly layered into the quintessential Italian comfort dish. Imagine wide, ruffled sheets of pasta, tender as satin, embracing rich, slow-simmered ragù—a deep, aromatic blend of savory ground meat, sweet San Marzano tomatoes, and a hint of garlic and herbs that has bubbled for hours until luxuriously thick. Between these layers lies the heart of the dish: creamy béchamel sauce, delicately infused with nutmeg, mingling with generous clouds of melted mozzarella and sharp, nutty Parmigiano-Reggiano. Each forkful delivers a harmonious balance—the slight resistance of the pasta, the hearty richness of the meat sauce, and the velvety, golden-cheese crust that crowns the top, baked until gloriously bubbly and speckled with bronze.

This is not merely baked pasta; it is a culinary embrace, designed to delight the senses and comfort the soul. The aroma alone—an intoxicating blend of toasted cheese, tomato, and basil—promises warmth and satisfaction. Served steaming from the oven, its layers hold their form yet melt seamlessly in the mouth, leaving a lingering richness that is both robust and refined. Perfect for gathering loved ones around the table, lasagna is a celebration of patience and care, a dish that tastes like home, no matter where you are.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings.
  3. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe.
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes.
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.
